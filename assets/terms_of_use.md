## 3. Prohibited Uses

### 3.1 Commercial Use

BSLVC data **may not** be used for:
- Commercial products or services
- Proprietary research for commercial gain
- Consulting services for profit
- Marketing or advertising purposes

**Exception**: Commercial use may be permitted with written authorization. Contact [licensing@university.de] for inquiries.

### 3.2 Participant Re-identification

Users **must not**:
- Attempt to identify individual participants
- Combine BSLVC data with other datasets to re-identify participants
- Contact participants based on information in the dataset
- Share any information that could compromise participant anonymity

### 3.3 Misrepresentation

Users **must not**:
- Misrepresent the source or nature of the data
- Claim authorship of BSLVC data
- Alter data in ways that misrepresent the original corpus
- Use data in misleading or deceptive contexts

## 5. Ethical Considerations

### 5.1 Participant Privacy

BSLVC data are **anonymized** to protect participant privacy. Users must:
- Maintain the anonymity of participants in all uses of the data
- Report only aggregate statistics when working with small subgroups
- Avoid publishing analyses that could enable participant identification

### 5.2 Minimum Cell Sizes

To protect privacy, when reporting statistics:
- **Minimum 10 participants** per group for descriptive statistics
- **Minimum 5 participants** per cell in cross-tabulations
- Suppress or aggregate cells with fewer participants

### 5.3 Sensitive Information

While demographic variables are included for research purposes, users should:
- Handle demographic information responsibly
- Avoid stereotyping or stigmatizing language
- Consider ethical implications of cross-tabulating sensitive variables

## 6. Data Integrity and Quality

### 6.1 Data Accuracy

BSLVC data are provided "as is." While we strive for accuracy:
- Users should verify data quality for their specific research needs
- Report any suspected data errors to [data-quality@university.de]
- Understand that acceptability judgments reflect participant perceptions, not objective grammaticality

### 6.2 Missing Data

BSLVC data contain missing values due to:
- Participant non-response
- Survey design (some items not applicable to all participants)
- Data collection issues

Users should:
- Handle missing data appropriately in their analyses
- Report missing data rates in publications
- Understand imputation methods used (see [Methodology](/methodology))

### 6.3 Data Updates and Versioning

The BSLVC corpus may be updated with:
- Additional participants
- Corrected coding errors
- New variables

For reproducibility:
- Note the version/date of data accessed
- Archive the specific dataset used in your analysis
- Check for updates before finalizing publications

## 7. Data Redistribution

### 7.1 Sharing Derived Datasets

Users may share **derived datasets** (e.g., filtered subsets, aggregated statistics) if:
- The original BSLVC source is cited
- The CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 license is maintained
- Participant anonymity is preserved
- Redistribution is for non-commercial purposes

### 7.2 Prohibited Redistribution

Users **may not**:
- Redistribute the complete BSLVC corpus independently
- Incorporate BSLVC data into commercial databases
- Sublicense BSLVC data under different terms

**Instead**: Direct others to the official BSLVC Dashboard to access data.
